Vesting - pick up your tokens every block

  • Schedule your ADRIA vesting every block.
  • You can claim the available amount at any time by clicking the "Claim ADRIA" button.
  • Once you open it, your tokens will be transferred to your wallet.
  • You can return each day to claim your newly acquired tokens (until your entire balance has been claimed).
Important: you will not be able to claim your tokens without clicking the "Claim ADRIA" button..
Note: please note that you must have BNB (BEP20) in your wallet in order to cover network fees for each ADRIA claim.
